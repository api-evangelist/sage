Senior living communities manage resident information across multiple disconnected systems, creating inefficiencies that drain staff time and obscure critical insights. Data integration connects clinical, operational, and financial platforms into a unified ecosystem that improves care coordination and provides leadership with actionable intelligence. Understanding how integration works and how to implement it successfully helps communities deliver better resident outcomes while optimizing operations.
