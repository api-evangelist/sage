Supply chain disruption refers to unexpected events that interrupt the flow of goods, materials, or information across operations. The article explains that disruptions stem from both internal factors like poor inventory management and disconnected systems, as well as external factors such as tariffs and natural disasters. To manage disruptions effectively, businesses should establish response teams, monitor their supply networks, and implement connected ERP systems like Sage X3 to improve visibility and enable faster decision-making across finance and operations.
