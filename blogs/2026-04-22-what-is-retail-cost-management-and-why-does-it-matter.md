Retail businesses operate on tight margins, where controlling costs directly impacts profitability and growth. Retail cost management provides a structured approach to track, analyze, and optimize spending across inventory, labor, overhead, and technology. By understanding cost categories and applying both direct and indirect strategies, retailers can improve efficiency, protect margins, and stay competitive.
